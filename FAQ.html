FAQs

  • Why don’t my recipes appear after I add them?

    Most of the time this happens if the page wasn’t fully loaded. Refresh the page and the recipe usually appears.

  • Why are prices not showing during checkout?

    The app pulls prices from outside sources. If those sources are slow, the prices may take a moment to load or may not load right away.

  • Why did my PDF download fail?

    If your internet connection is slow or a page element didn’t load, the PDF may fail. Refreshing the page fixes it.

  • Why can’t I save or open my itineraries?

    This usually happens if you’ve been logged in for a long time. Logging out and back in fixes it.
